**Procurement and Contracting Terms.** In the event the Customer decides to proceed with a commercial license, the Customer agrees to work with their internal procurement or legal teams to ensure the removal or exclusion of the following from the final agreement:

- Any clauses pertaining to professional services or service components, as the transaction constitutes a software license and not a service engagement;
- Any clauses related to data security or data protection, as the Company does not process or access Customer data;
- Any clauses specific to cloud services, SaaS, or hosted solutions, as the Product is strictly deployed on-premises.
