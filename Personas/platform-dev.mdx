You maybe building a platform or part of team that is creating a synthetic data module in a larger data platform. Here are the insights we have gathered from our users who use SDV Community and integrate with their platform. 

You are essentially creating a startup within a large enterprise. The first and foremost thing you must realize is that you are creating a startup within a large enterprise. You are bringing a deep AI technology into an enterprise and attempting to scale its adoption. So all the aspects that come with creating and having users adopt a new technology will come into play. You will have to do marketing, sales, advocacy along side building the platform and tooling. 

Choose the right partner for the synthetic data software. You must choose the right partner as your synthetic data generation software provider. The partner must meet many of the requirements that are seen in the enterprise data. On the surface, it may appear that you don't need those requirements to be met, and you can postpone the selection till a later date, but in doing so, you are severely limiting the user uptake, success and here is why
