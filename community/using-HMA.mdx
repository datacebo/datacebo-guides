When you begin working with SDV Community, the primary tool for modeling multi-table datasets is the HMASynthesizer. _HMA_ stands for Hierarchical Modeling Algorithm, a synthesizer originally designed to generate high-fidelity synthetic data. It works by learning a separate model for each grouping within a multi-table structure, allowing it to capture relationships and dependencies across tables.
